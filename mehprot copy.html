About Me

Farzia Tabassum

Professional Bridal Henna Artist | Chennai

With over 7 years of professional expertise, I am proud to be recognized as one of Chennai's premier bridal henna artists. My work is a heartfelt fusion of tradition, artistry, and elegance—crafted to reflect the individuality of each bride I have the honor of adorning.

I specialize in custom, intricate bridal mehndi using 100% organic, homemade henna, carefully prepared with natural ingredients to ensure deep, long-lasting stains and a safe, skin-friendly application. Every paste is crafted fresh, ensuring not only rich color but also a soothing and aromatic experience.

Whether it's a grand wedding celebration, an intimate engagement, or a festive occasion like Eid, Karva Chauth, or Baby Showers, my designs are personalized to match your style and significance of the moment. From traditional Rajasthani and Mughal patterns to modern minimalistic themes, I blend classical motifs with contemporary elements to suit your preferences.
